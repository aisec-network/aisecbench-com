AI Sec Bench publishes independent, reproducible benchmarks of AI security tooling — detection rates, false positive rates, latency under load, and integration complexity, tested against real-world workloads and public attack libraries.

## What we benchmark

- **Detection rate testing** — measured against real jailbreak and adversarial input corpora, not synthetic datasets
- **False positive and negative analysis** — where tools over- and under-trigger, and the operational impact
- **Performance under load** — latency, throughput, and degradation patterns at production scale
- **Integration complexity** — time-to-working, configuration surface, and operational overhead
- **Vendor comparison** — honest side-by-side scoring with documented methodology and caveats

## Who reads this

Security engineers, ML platform teams, and procurement professionals evaluating AI security products. Benchmarks are reproducible — test harnesses are published alongside results, and datasets are documented.

## Stay current

Subscribe to the RSS feed for new benchmark releases. Test harnesses are open for review — if you find a methodological error, open a correction request via the editorial desk.
